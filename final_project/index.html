About Us




"She Fights" is an innovative app dedicated to enhancing women's safety by providing essential tools for protection and security.

The app offers real-time tracking features that allow trusted contacts to monitor the user's location, ensuring that help is always nearby when needed. It also includes safe zone identification, helping users find and navigate to nearby secure areas.

Additionally, users can set up emergency contacts to be alerted instantly in case of danger, making "She Fights" a comprehensive solution designed to empower women and promote their safety and well-being in everyday life.
